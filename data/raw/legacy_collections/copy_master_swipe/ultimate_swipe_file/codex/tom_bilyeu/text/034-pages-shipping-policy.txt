Shipping / FAQs

Estimated shipping delivery times

USA: 3-5 business days

Rest of the World: 5-10 business days

Your order will be sent out on average within 1-5 business days of ordering. Expedited shipping options available at checkout.

Costs vary on the shipping option you pick at checkout.

International shipping

International shipments may incur customs fees. For each country the customs policy is different, and the fee is usually based on a variety of factors like weight, value, and size.
We do not take responsibility for customs fees.

Tracking options

Orders within the United States by USPS have full tracking available. For international orders, full tracking will only be available for orders sent through International Priority, which costs a bit more.

Order never arrived

If your order doesn't arrive, be sure to let us know! 

First, check your shipping confirmation and check that you had entered the correct address. If the address was incorrect, though we'd be happy to send you another order to the correct address, it will have to be at your cost.

If the shipping address was correct, get in touch with us at shop@impacttheory.com noting your order number. We'll re-send your order on us!

Returns policy

Unsatisfied items

We offer a 7 Day Hassle FREE Returns if you’re unsatisfied with your item. Please contact us at shop@impacttheory.com. 

Damaged items
The last thing we want is for you to be stuck with a damaged item. We offer 30 days FREE Returns/Exchanges for any defected items from us. 

Wrong size
If you're unhappy with the size you've received, first check that the size stated on the garment matches the size stated on the sizing chart. Though rare, it's possible that the garment was mislabelled. Let us know and we'll get you sorted out. 

If you'd like a different size, get in touch with us at shop@impacttheory.com and we'll let you know what your next steps are.
