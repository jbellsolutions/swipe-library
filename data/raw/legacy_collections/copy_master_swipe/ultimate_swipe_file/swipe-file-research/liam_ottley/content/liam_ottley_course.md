## Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the AAA Accelerator?+

The AAA Accelerator is a comprehensive program that gives you everything you need to build a profitable AI automation agency — a structured classroom curriculum, unlimited 1-on-1 coaching across development, sales, outreach, personal branding, and business strategy, plus a community of 3,800+ fellow AI entrepreneurs.

Do I need technical experience to join?+

No. Our curriculum starts from the foundations, and you get unlimited 1-on-1 development coaching to help you through every technical challenge. Many of our most successful students started with zero coding experience.

How much time do I need to commit?+

Most successful students commit 10-20 hours per week. The program is self-paced with on-demand coaching, so you can fit it around a full-time job if needed. Your dedicated Success Manager will help you create a realistic timeline.

How is this different from free YouTube content?+

YouTube gives you fragments. The Accelerator gives you the complete system — structured curriculum, personalized coaching, proven sales frameworks, outreach templates, and a direct line to people who've already built what you're trying to build. Plus accountability to make sure you actually execute.

What kind of results can I expect?+

Results depend on your effort and execution. Our students have built agencies generating anywhere from their first few thousand dollars to $30K+/month. The Accelerator provides the system, coaching, and support — you provide the work.

What does the coaching actually look like?+

You get unlimited 1-on-1 calls with our full coaching team — development coaches, sales coaches, outreach specialists, personal branding experts, business strategists, and a dedicated Success Manager. Plus weekly group coaching calls directly with Liam.

What happens on the strategy call?+

The strategy call is a free, no-pressure conversation where we learn about your goals, assess if the Accelerator is the right fit for you, and map out what your first 90 days would look like. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you honestly.

Is there a community or am I on my own?+

You're joining a community of 3,800+ AI entrepreneurs worldwide. This is one of the most active and supportive communities in the AI space — members share wins, help each other with challenges, and collaborate on projects daily.
