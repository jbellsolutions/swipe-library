Structure (Todd Brown + Alex Hormozi DNA)

LinkedIn Post — Midday Value Drop LINKEDIN

Structure (Brian Kurtz + Jay Abraham DNA)

STORY LEAD (2-3 lines): [Open with a specific moment — "Last Tuesday, I was [doing X] when..."] [Create tension or curiosity] LESSON (4-6 lines): [What the story taught you] [Frame as universal principle, not just personal win] [Use Jay Abraham's "strategy of preeminence" angle — position reader's success above your own] ACTIONABLE TAKEAWAY (2-3 lines): [Exact steps they can implement TODAY] [Be specific — numbers, tools, frameworks] CTA: [Save this ♻️ if you found it valuable] [OR: "DM me [KEYWORD] if you want my [RESOURCE]"]

LinkedIn Newsletter (Daily) NEWSLETTER

Structure (Jay Abraham Long-Form + Tom Bilyeu Narrative)

HEADLINE: [How [PERSON/COMPANY] [achieved RESULT] by [doing COUNTERINTUITIVE THING]] [OR: The [NUMBER]-Step [FRAMEWORK NAME] for [DESIRED OUTCOME]] INTRO (2-3 paragraphs): [Start with a story or shocking stat tied to CURRENT TREND] [Establish why this matters RIGHT NOW] [Preview the value: "In this issue, you'll learn..."] SECTION 1: THE PROBLEM [Describe the pain point your audience faces] [Use specific numbers and scenarios] SECTION 2: THE FRAMEWORK/SOLUTION [Your unique methodology — give it a name] [Break into 3-5 clear steps] [Include real examples or case studies] SECTION 3: IMPLEMENTATION [Exact steps to apply this TODAY] [Tools, scripts, templates if applicable] CLOSE: [Recap the key insight] [CTA to your offer/calendar/lead magnet] [Ask readers to share if valuable]

Daily Production Workflow

Step-by-step process that runs every day to produce all 7 content pieces

1

Trend Scrape (6:00 AM Auto)

Scrape top trending topics from: LinkedIn Trending, Google Trends, X/Twitter, and industry-specific sources. Filter for relevance to your niche. Output: 5-10 trending topics ranked by relevance.

2

Swipe File Match (6:15 AM Auto)

Cross-reference trends with the 5,716-email swipe file. Find copywriting frameworks, hooks, and structures that map to today's trending topics. Pull the best-matching email structures as templates.

3

Content Generation (6:30 AM Auto)

Generate all 7 pieces using: trending topic + swipe file framework + your business context + your brand voice. Each piece follows the channel-specific template with DR copywriting DNA baked in.

4

Quality Review (6:45 AM — Optional Human Review)

Quick scan of generated content. Approve, edit, or regenerate any piece. Can be fully automated once you trust the output quality.

5

Publish: Beehiiv Newsletter (8:00 AM)

Push daily newsletter to Beehiiv. Subject line uses the 63% curiosity formula. Body follows the story-driven Bill Mueller template.

6

Publish: LinkedIn Content (7AM / 12PM / 5PM)

Three posts scheduled across the day. Newsletter and article published at 9AM and 10AM. Each post uses different DR copywriting DNA for variety.

7

Publish: ActiveCampaign Autoresponder (Evergreen)

Daily email added to the rotating 7-day sequence. Builds your autoresponder library over time. Each new email follows the day's position in the cycle (story, value, proof, objection, hot take, soft pitch, hard pitch).

8

Performance Tracking (End of Day Auto)

Pull engagement metrics across all channels. Track: open rates, click rates, LinkedIn impressions/engagement, newsletter growth. Feed winning patterns back into the swipe file for continuous improvement.
