# The last 10% is everything - Brian Kurtz

This article discusses the author's perspective on AI, emphasizing that while AI can achieve 90% of a task, the final 10%—the human touch, instinct, ingenuity, and intellectual curiosity—is crucial. He uses AI for creating interview questions, drafting blog posts (with a 10% human touch), and flattery, but not for storytelling or humor. He also highlights the importance of core marketing fundamentals like the 41/39/20 Rule, Customer Lifetime Value (LTV), and Recency, Frequency, Monetary (RFM) analysis.
