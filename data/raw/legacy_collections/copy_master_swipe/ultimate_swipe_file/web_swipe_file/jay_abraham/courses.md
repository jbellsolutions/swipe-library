# Jay Abraham Courses

Jay Abraham offers a variety of courses and training programs focused on business growth, marketing, and strategy. His teachings often emphasize "multiplicative thinking" and identifying hidden opportunities within a business.

## Notable courses and programs include:

*   **"Creating Your Own Business Success"**: A home study course designed to instill an entrepreneurial mindset and teach how to build a successful business with minimal capital or risk.
*   **"Breakthrough Thinking Masterclass"**: This program focuses on identifying and activating breakthrough opportunities across various aspects of a revenue system, utilizing frameworks like "The Multiplicative Thinking" framework.
*   **"Referral Mastery System"**: A training course dedicated to generating high-value referrals, teaching 150 different strategies and the psychology behind referral generation.
*   **"Deal Makers Course"**: An annual course, with a "Deal Makers Course 2025" being one example.

Abraham's programs cover a wide range of topics, including marketing strategy, business models, competitive superiority, distribution, channel expansion, strategic alliances, and joint ventures. He also teaches concepts like "The Strategy of Preeminence," which aims to position a business as the most trusted advisor in its field. While some high-level private consultations have been valued at $250,000, some masterclasses are offered at no cost to qualified leaders, and other programs like the "Referral Mastery System" are available for around $1,995.
