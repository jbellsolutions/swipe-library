What actually happens during the 2 days?

It's not an event. It's a workshop. At my office.

Expect zero motivational talks. Meaning - we get tactical.

On day one, we break down how we fast track growth, recruit top talent, and frameworks we use for "high return" decision making.

This is the same stuff we use in our portfolio.

On day two, we get hands on. We separate into small groups.

You meet with our directors (specialized in marketing, sales, people, profit, and strategy) to get personalized help.

What do I walk away with?

The aim: you walk away with 3-5 tactical next steps.

You spend time with our team. We tailor the advice to your business size, industry, goals, and leadership style.

It's hands-on help specific to your biggest challenge.

Plus; you get a thick packet w/ key takeaways & strategies.

Is this right for my business size/industry?

Size: If you are under 250k/yr this is not a fit (yet.)

If you are between $1m-$100m/yr, then we can help. How is it possible to have such a wide range?

Answer: everything implementation wise is separated by revenue.

Meaning - you will be paired with businesses around your size, so we can get tactical.

Industry: To make this easier, I attached a list of all the industries that we've recently worked with:

Real estate, Medical, Auto, Home owners, Insurance, Healthcare, Local business, Home services, Financial services, Consumer services, B2B services, B2B Software, B2C Software, Apps, E-learning, Online businesses, and Ecommerce.

If you are one of these, chances are we can help.

How much are tickets?

Tickets are $5k per seat. This allows us to:

1) keep the room quality high (meet & network with real business owners doing real business)

2) give you access to top talent (you work with our directors that are paid $350k, $600k, $900k+ per year)
