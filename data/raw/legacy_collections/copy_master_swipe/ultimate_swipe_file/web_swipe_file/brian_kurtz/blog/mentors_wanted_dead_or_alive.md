# Mentors Wanted...Dead or Alive - Brian Kurtz

This article focuses on the importance of mentors and learning from experienced individuals. The author believes that mentors choose you, rather than the other way around. He discusses the value of learning from "results leaders" who possess wisdom gained from years of experience and failures. He also emphasizes the responsibility of documenting and sharing knowledge from mentors, both living and deceased, to benefit future generations. He mentions creating custom AI bots loaded with the genius of his mentors to keep their ideas alive.
