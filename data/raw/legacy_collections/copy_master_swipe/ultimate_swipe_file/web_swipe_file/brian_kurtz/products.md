# Brian Kurtz Products

This page lists various products offered by Brian Kurtz, primarily focused on direct response marketing education and resources.

## Overdeliver: Build a Business for a Lifetime Playing the Long Game in Direct Response Marketing by Brian Kurtz

This book provides a roadmap to grow a business, make more money, and maximize impact. It's for both new and seasoned marketers. Purchasing the book also grants access to The Overdeliver Collection, a collection of direct response marketing materials.

## Breakthrough Advertising by Eugene M. Schwartz

Described as the most important book ever written about persuasion, copywriting, marketing, and human behavior. Brian Kurtz's website is presented as the only place to buy this classic at the lowest price available.

## The Brilliance Breakthrough: How To Talk And Write So That People Will Never Forget You by Eugene M. Schwartz

This book teaches how to write "word pictures" to make copy more persuasive. It comes with a workbook for practical exercises.

## Titans of Direct Response (12 video discs, swipe files, interviews from the 2014 event)

A product packaging a legendary 2014 event where direct response masters like Dan Kennedy, Gary Bencivenga, Joe Sugarman, and Jay Abraham shared their insights. It's described as a collector's item and a source of eternal lessons.

## Read this or Die. The Lost Files of Jim Rutz

A collection of private swipe files, controls, and other copy from Jim Rutz, one of the greatest copywriters. It includes interviews, video, and never-before-seen correspondence.

## The Bill Jayme Collection

A USB thumb drive containing every direct mail package Bill Jayme ever wrote—210 individual direct mail efforts in PDF format for 138 different mailers. It also includes a bonus of Bill himself presenting live.

## The Advertising Solution: Influence Prospects, Multiply Sales and Promote Your Brand by Craig Simpson with Brian Kurtz

Brian's first book, co-authored with direct mail expert Craig Simpson, is a reference and resource book for direct marketers, copywriters, practitioners, and students.
