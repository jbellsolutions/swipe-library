# Tom Bilyeu Sales Page - Zero to Founder Program

Tom Bilyeu is known for his "Zero to Founder" program, which is a monthly subscription service designed to help aspiring entrepreneurs build their businesses. This program is offered through the platform Skool.

## Key aspects of the "Zero to Founder" program:

*   **Purpose:** Designed to guide aspiring entrepreneurs through launching their businesses.
*   **Pricing:** $119 per month.
*   **Content:** Includes a 4-week curriculum, coaching, access to a community, and custom AI tools.
*   **Foundation:** Draws on Tom Bilyeu's experience as a co-founder of Quest Nutrition.

Additionally, Tom Bilyeu offers an "AI Masterclass" titled "Leverage AI to Launch Your Business," which aims to teach attendees how to use AI tools like Claude or ChatGPT to build a business within 72 hours, even without prior experience.
