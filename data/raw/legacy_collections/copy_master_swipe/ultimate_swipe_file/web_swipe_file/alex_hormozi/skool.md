# Alex Hormozi - Skool and Skool Games

## Skool Platform

Skool is an all-in-one online platform designed for creators, coaches, and community builders. It integrates online courses, community forums, and calendars to facilitate learning and engagement. Alex Hormozi is a co-founder and major investor in Skool.

## Skool Games

The Skool Games is a monthly challenge or tournament that began in February 2024. It's a business-building competition aimed at helping entrepreneurs and content creators establish, expand, and monetize their online communities.

### How it Works

Participants create communities on the Skool platform and compete to attract paying members. A leaderboard tracks participants based on the monthly recurring revenue generated from their communities.

### Prizes

The competition offers various prizes, including one-on-one time with Alex Hormozi, a trip to Las Vegas to meet him, and, during specific promotional periods, a Tesla Cybertruck for top performers.

### Alex Hormozi's Involvement

As part of the Skool Games, Alex Hormozi provides an exclusive course focused on community building and monetization strategies. The initiative is designed to help individuals overcome common challenges in starting an online business, such as identifying what to sell, building an audience, or lacking expertise.
