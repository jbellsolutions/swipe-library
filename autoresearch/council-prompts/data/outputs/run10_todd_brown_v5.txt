1. IS THERE A MECHANISM? A specific, named element that explains WHY this solution works.

5. IS IT PROVEN? Data, case studies, demonstrations, before/after results.
6. IS IT REVEALED STRATEGICALLY? Top of funnel = curiosity. Middle = belief. Bottom = commitment.
