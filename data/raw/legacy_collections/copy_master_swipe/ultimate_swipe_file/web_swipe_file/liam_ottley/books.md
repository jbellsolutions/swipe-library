# Liam Ottley Books (eBooks)

Liam Ottley has authored at least two eBooks:

*   **"How to Build Your AI Agency"**: This eBook, published on December 15, 2024, is part of the "Master AI in 2025 and Beyond" series. It aims to guide readers in creating a profitable AI business from scratch, covering five proven business models.
*   **"THE ULTIMATE AI ROADMAP FOR DEVELOPERS"**: Also part of the "Master AI in 2025 and Beyond" series, this eBook was published on December 22, 2024. It serves as a guide for developers to navigate the AI landscape and build a future-proof career.

Additionally, there are documents available such as "The Ottley Playbook" and a "6-Month AI Automation Agency Roadmap," which provide insights into entrepreneurial resilience in AI and a step-by-step guide for starting an AI Automation Agency, respectively.
