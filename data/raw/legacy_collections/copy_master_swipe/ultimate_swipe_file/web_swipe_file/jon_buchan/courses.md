# Jon Buchan Courses

Jon Buchan is known for his "Charm Offensive" approach to copywriting and marketing, which emphasizes using humor and radical honesty in business communication, particularly in cold emails.

## He offers several courses and resources related to his Charm Offensive methodology:

*   **Charm Offensive Pro 2022**: This is a comprehensive course designed to transform business communications and marketing. It includes resources, templates, training, and eBooks covering marketing, lead generation, and client interactions.
*   **The Everything I've Ever Made Stash**: This offering includes training on how Jon Buchan launched an ebook, key questions for creating captivating content, "media hacking" tactics, using LinkedIn for lead generation, building influencer relationships, and making every interaction matter. It also covers "The Charming Open for Business Pack," which details platforms and strategies for running a successful business selling information products.
*   **My Charming Cold Pitch Mini-Course**: This mini-course focuses on the crucial variables behind cold pitching campaigns.

Buchan's approach stems from an accidental breakthrough where a "drunk cold email" he sent led to significant business opportunities. He now teaches others how to apply his unique blend of humor and candor to get better results in copywriting and marketing.
