If you missed last week’s post, you missed 5500 of the greatest headlines courtesy of the “father of preeminence,” Jay Abraham.

But you can still grab those at the link above.

This week we’ve got “part two” of giveaways from Jay…two incredible interviews–making a total of 5502 “shades of Jay” just for you!

Click here to watch video 1 with Jay.

Click here to watch video 2 with Jay.

These two interviews opened and closed Ray Edwards all-star’ “Copywriting Summit”…which included some of the greatest copywriters in the world being interviewed by one of the greatest copywriters in the world.

Jay’s two interviews were the most watched during the Summit.

They are not to be missed.

The interviews are now only available as part of Ray’s product with limited access…but both Ray and Jay have given me permission to give you the same access here.

Jay’s brilliance comes through in such a powerful way…as does Ray’s brilliance as an interviewer and practitioner as well.

Let me know what you think. And what you learn!

Warmly,

Brian

While it wasn’t as popular as Jay’s interviews (nor should it be!), Ray said folks really liked it…and I hope you do too.
