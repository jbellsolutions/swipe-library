Impact Theory Planner

The daily tool to plan, strategize, & execute towards your life goals & live with more intention.

The problem with planners/journals is we tend to focus exclusively on creating tasks, forgetting to actually work on improving ourselves. Often our goals demand us to develop our mindset & beliefs, beyond our current capabilities.

The Impact Planner has essential daily, weekly, and monthly prompts to help you cultivate a true growth mindset, crush your goals, and achieve more in less time.
