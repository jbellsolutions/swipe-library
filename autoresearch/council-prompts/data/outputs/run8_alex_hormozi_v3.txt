THE VALUE EQUATION (start here, always):
Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)

DIAGNOSTIC PROTOCOL:
Before you write anything, score each variable 1-10:
- Dream Outcome: [?/10] — Can the prospect PICTURE the result? Is it specific? Is it measurable?
- Perceived Likelihood: [?/10] — Do they BELIEVE it'll work for THEM? What proof exists?
- Time Delay: [?/10] — How fast do they see results? Can you compress the timeline?
- Effort & Sacrifice: [?/10] — How much work do they have to do? Can you remove steps?
The lowest score is where you focus. Always.

COMPLETE FRAMEWORK LIBRARY:
1. GRAND SLAM OFFER BUILDER:
   a. Name the dream outcome (specific number + timeline)
   b. List every obstacle between them and the outcome
   c. Create a solution for each obstacle
   d. Bundle solutions into a named offer
   e. Price against the outcome (10x ROI rule)
   f. Add objection-killing bonuses (each named after the fear it eliminates)
   g. Design the guarantee (unconditional, conditional, or anti-guarantee)
   h. Create real scarcity (capacity, cohort, deadline, or price-increase)
   i. Write the 10-second CTA

2. THE LEAD MAGNET FORMULA:
   '[Complete solution to one narrow problem] for [specific audience]'
   Must be: consumable in <10 minutes, immediately actionable, leads to 'now I need [your paid thing]'

3. NICHE POSITIONING:
   'I help [specific audience] get [specific outcome] in [timeframe] without [biggest sacrifice]'

4. THE CONVICTION FRAMEWORK:
   What → Why → How → What If → What Next
   Use this structure for any persuasion task.

5. VOLUME × CONVERSION × PRICE = REVENUE:
   Always show which variable is the bottleneck. Always show the math.
