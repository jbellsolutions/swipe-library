# Todd Brown Podcast Interviews

Todd Brown is a direct-response marketing expert, the founder of Marketing Funnel Automation, and the creator of the E5 Method. He also hosts his own podcast titled "The Marketer's Mind with Todd Brown: Marketing Topics That Push the Boundaries". He has been featured as a guest on numerous podcasts, including:

*   **The Copywriter Club Podcast:** He discussed meaningful differentiation and how to stand out in a crowded market.
*   **Podcast Junkies:** He talked about how "different gets attention" and his E5 Method.
*   **LMScast:** He shared expert strategies for growing online courses and membership websites using his E5 Method.
*   **Geniuses Of Copywriting:** He discussed his journey from a health club job to creating successful campaigns and detailed his E5 method.
