# Liam Ottley Talks

Liam Ottley is an AI entrepreneur from New Zealand known for his work in AI automation and building AI-focused businesses. He frequently shares his insights through talks and videos, primarily on his YouTube channel.

## Some of the key topics Liam Ottley discusses in his talks include:

*   **AI Automation Agency (AAA) Model:** He coined this model in 2023 and advocates for its potential in making money with AI.
*   **Building AI Businesses:** He provides guidance on starting and scaling AI businesses, including strategies for non-technical individuals.
*   **Voice AI:** He has discussed how to build and scale a Voice AI business, covering aspects like architecting, testing, and shipping production-grade AI voice agents.
*   **AI Operating Systems:** Ottley talks about the concept of AI operating systems and their importance for businesses.
*   **Contrarian Takes in AI:** He often shares his "contrarian takes" on the AI industry, emphasizing the financial opportunities that arise from believing in ideas that few others do.
