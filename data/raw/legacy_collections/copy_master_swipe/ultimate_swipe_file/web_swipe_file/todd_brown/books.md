# Todd Brown Books

Todd Brown has authored several marketing books, focusing on engineering profitable customer acquisition campaigns and marketing strategies.

## Notable works include:

*   **"The Big Marketing Idea Book" (also known as "How To Find Your Big Marketing Idea: Standout & Grab Attention With Your Marketing Message In A Crowded Marketplace")**: This book focuses on finding new customers, creating captivating marketing messages, and the "Big Marketing Idea" concept.
*   **"Great Little Marketing Book"**: This book also focuses on marketing strategies.
