# Todd Brown Sales Page Philosophy

Todd Brown is recognized as a leading authority in engineering profitable customer acquisition campaigns, with many experts seeking his guidance for their own marketing strategies. He has been instrumental in helping his students develop numerous six and seven-figure marketing campaigns.

## While direct examples of sales pages *authored* by Todd Brown are not readily available in the search results, his influence on successful sales page strategies is highlighted:

*   **EPIC Email Sales Page:** Todd Brown collaborated with Jon Benson on the "EPIC Email" sales page, contributing to its success by refining elements like the pre-header and headline to make claims more believable and benefits more dimensional. He emphasized creating a "make-different" offer rather than just a "make-better" one.
*   **Sales Page Principles:** Brown advocates for rigorous testing of every element within a sales funnel, stressing that what works in one context may not work in another. He also suggests that even opt-in pages should be treated as "sales pages" because they aim to "sell" the prospect on the value of exchanging their information for a lead magnet. The true "bribe" on an opt-in page is the perceived "change in circumstance" the prospect will experience after consuming the lead magnet.
*   **Retargeting Strategies:** Todd Brown utilizes personalized copy in Facebook retargeting campaigns, referencing specific pages and actions tracked from previous visits. This approach aims to "remind" and "encourage" past visitors to revisit an offer rather than trying to "sell them" from scratch.

For those looking to understand his approach, his work often emphasizes the importance of testing, understanding the "big promise" of an offer, and crafting compelling copy that focuses on dimensional benefits and presupposition.
