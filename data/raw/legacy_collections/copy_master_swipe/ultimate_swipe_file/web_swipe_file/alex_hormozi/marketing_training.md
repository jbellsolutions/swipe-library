# Alex Hormozi Marketing Training

Alex Hormozi offers various marketing training resources, primarily through his company, Acquisition.com. These resources include free online courses, advanced training materials, and in-person workshops.

## Free Courses

Acquisition.com provides free access to several courses, including:
*   **$100M Money Models**
*   **Offer Creation Course**
*   **Lead Generation Course**
*   **Scaling Course**

## Scaling Workshop

This is a two-day, live, in-person event held at Acquisition.com's headquarters. It focuses on tactical strategies for business growth, talent recruitment, and high-return decision-making. Attendance requires meeting specific minimum revenue requirements, typically for businesses earning between $3 million and $150 million annually.

## Books

Hormozi is the author of two best-selling books, "$100M Offers" and "$100M Leads," which are prominent in the marketing and sales categories on Amazon. These books outline his philosophies on sales and marketing.

## Marketing Principles

Hormozi's training often emphasizes principles such as focusing on a single channel, customer avatar, and product until reaching significant revenue milestones, prioritizing tangible proof over promises, and strategically increasing pricing.
