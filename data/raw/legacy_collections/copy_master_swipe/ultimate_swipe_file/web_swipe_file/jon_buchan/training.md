# Jon Buchan Training

Jon Buchan offers a variety of training programs centered around his "Charm Offensive" methodology, which focuses on using humor and radical candor in business communication and marketing, particularly for cold outreach. His approach aims to help individuals and businesses stand out and generate leads by crafting engaging and unconventional messages.

## Key training programs and areas of focus include:

*   **Charm Offensive Pro 2022**: A comprehensive course that covers business communications and marketing, offering resources, templates, and eBooks. It includes over 250 ready-to-use templates, unorthodox lead generation techniques, and strategies for client interaction.
*   **Always Be Winning**: This program teaches how to develop a compelling offering, identify ideal clients, and craft a story and pitch to succeed without relying on websites or social media.
*   **Disarm With Charm**: This training focuses on finding ideal clients online, obtaining their contact details, and writing effective "Charm Offensive" copy to elicit responses.
*   **Build Your Influence**: This course guides participants on how to build an online presence using cold pitching tactics.
*   **Sell**: This training covers productizing expertise, developing initial products, and launching courses.
*   **Have Your Way With Words**: Designed for improving writing skills, this course includes a "humour writing" template pack for various content types like articles, emails, and social media, along with an introduction to the fundamentals of comedy.
*   **LinkedIn Charm Offensive**: This program focuses on leveraging LinkedIn to connect with prospects, send direct messages, engage with content, and utilize techniques like the "Charming Open Letter Technique."
*   **Charm Offensive Coaching**: This offers guidance on initiating conversations with ideal clients through direct mail and cold email, and building influence through cold-pitching.
*   **Jon's Big Fire Nation Moment Pack**: This resource provides access to joke formulas and rhetorical devices.
