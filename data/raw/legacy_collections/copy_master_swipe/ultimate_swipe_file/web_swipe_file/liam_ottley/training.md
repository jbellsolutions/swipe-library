# Liam Ottley Training

Liam Ottley offers various training programs and resources focused on building and scaling AI automation agencies. His offerings range from free content to a high-ticket mentorship program.

## Key training programs and resources include:

*   **AAA Accelerator**: This is a paid, six-month mentorship program designed to help individuals launch, sell, and scale AI automation agencies. It includes unlimited one-on-one coaching with specialists in development, sales, personal branding, outreach, and business strategy, as well as weekly coaching sessions directly with Liam Ottley. The program features a structured curriculum with 10 courses covering AI agents, workflow automation, sales psychology, client acquisition, and business operations.
*   **AIOS Blueprint Workshop**: A replay of a workshop is available, which teaches how to build an AI Operating System for businesses.
*   **AI Automation Agency Hub (Skool Community)**: A free community that provides a complete AI Automation Agency Course, weekly live Q&A sessions with Liam Ottley, templates, resources, and access to a network of AI professionals.
*   **Free YouTube Courses**: Liam Ottley offers extensive free video courses on his YouTube channel, such as "The Ultimate AI Agency Masterclass (6+ HOUR FREE COURSE)" and "Start an Ai Agency in 2025 - FULL COURSE (3 hours)". These cover topics like starting an AI business, developing AI skills, client acquisition, and various AI automation strategies.
*   **6-Month AI Automation Agency Roadmap**: This free resource provides a step-by-step guide for beginners to establish and grow an AI Automation Agency, detailing phases for learning, applying skills, and monetizing services.

It's important to note that some reviews have raised concerns regarding the high cost, sales tactics, and refund policy of the AAA Accelerator program.
