# The Bot who didn't come to dinner - Brian Kurtz

This is a thought experiment where the author uses ChatGPT to generate a blog post about "Boardroom/Intentional Dinners." The post, partially AI-generated, emphasizes the value of human-to-human interaction, real access, and unfiltered conversations in these dinners, contrasting them with the limitations of AI in replicating such experiences. The author notes that while AI can do justice to the topic, it lacks heart and humor.
