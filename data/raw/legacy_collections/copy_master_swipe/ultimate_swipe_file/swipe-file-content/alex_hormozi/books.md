# Alex Hormozi Books

## "$100M Offers: How To Make Offers So Good People Feel Stupid Saying No"

This business book provides entrepreneurs with strategies to enhance the value of their products or services, aiming to increase demand, allow for higher pricing, and ultimately boost profit.

The book emphasizes creating a "category of one" for a business and offers practical advice on choosing a market, understanding value, and incorporating bonuses and guarantees into offers. Hormozi also discusses a "Value Equation" to help businesses understand how to make their offers more appealing.

## "$100M Leads: How to Get Strangers To Want To Buy Your Stuff"

Published around August 2023, the book provides strategies and playbooks for businesses to generate leads. Hormozi shares insights from his experience, detailing how he scaled his companies to generate significant revenue. The book covers topics such as the principles of advertising, four core methods for acquiring leads, and how to leverage others (employees, customers, affiliates, and agencies) to generate leads. It also emphasizes the importance of creating valuable "lead magnets" and offers actionable advice for entrepreneurs and business owners. "$100M Leads" is part of a series that includes "$100M Offers" and "$100M Money Models".
