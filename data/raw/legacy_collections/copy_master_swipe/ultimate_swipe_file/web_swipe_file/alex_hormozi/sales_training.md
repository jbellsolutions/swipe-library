# Alex Hormozi Sales Training

Alex Hormozi's sales training emphasizes a philosophy centered on providing immense value, building trust, and guiding prospects to make beneficial decisions, rather than simply pushing products. His approach is detailed across various free resources, including his books and online courses.

## Key principles and frameworks of Alex Hormozi's sales training include:

*   **The Offer is King:** Hormozi stresses the importance of crafting irresistible offers that clearly articulate the "dream outcome" for the customer, a concept he elaborates on in his book "$100M Offers."
*   **Give, Give, Give, Ask:** He advocates for a "long-term greedy" strategy, where businesses provide significant value upfront to build trust and rapport before making a sales pitch.
*   **CLOSER Framework:** This is a structured approach to sales conversations:
    *   **C**larify why they're there.
    *   **L**abel them with a problem.
    *   **O**verview their past pain.
    *   **S**ell them a vacation (focus on the desired outcome, not just the features).
    *   **E**xplain away their concerns.
    *   **R**einforce their decision.
*   **Conviction and Belief:** Sales is viewed as the "transference of belief over a bridge of trust." Salespeople must genuinely believe in their product or service to effectively convey its value.
*   **Be a Coach, Not a Salesperson:** The sales process should involve coaching prospects to make the right decision for themselves, even if that means not buying the product.
*   **Daily Training:** Hormozi advocates for consistent daily practice, often suggesting 60 minutes a day, five days a week, to refine communication skills, tonality, and objection handling.
*   **Record and Evaluate Sales Calls:** Regularly reviewing sales calls is crucial for identifying areas of improvement and continuous learning.
*   **Handle Obstacles Before Objections:** He differentiates between obstacles (pre-sale concerns) and objections (concerns after the offer is presented), emphasizing the need to address obstacles proactively.
*   **Speed of Response:** Rapid response to leads, ideally within 60 seconds, is highlighted as a significant factor in increasing closing rates.
