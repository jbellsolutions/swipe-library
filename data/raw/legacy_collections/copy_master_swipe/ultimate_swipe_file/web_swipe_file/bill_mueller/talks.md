# Bill Mueller Talks

## "The Story Copywriter Podcast" - Episode 58: "Email Storytelling Insights"

### Key topics covered include:

*   Why email offers the highest return on investment for storytelling.
*   Balancing automated and broadcast emails.
*   Repurposing old content.
*   Making writing entertaining, including the use of humor.
*   Whether writing should always be "on topic."
*   The ideal length and frequency of emails.
*   Finding inspiration for stories.
*   The under-utilization of images in email.
*   The importance of journaling.
*   Tips for hooking readers and keeping them engaged.
*   Whether to include a call to action in every email.

Bill Mueller, an email copywriter and founder of The Story Sales Machine, shared his expertise, drawing from his background in journalism and direct marketing principles. He also recommended books such as "Winning the Story Wars" by Jonah Sachs, "Storyworthy" by Matthew Dicks, and "Story" by Robert McKee.
