# Liam Ottley Courses

Liam Ottley offers courses primarily focused on helping individuals start and scale AI automation agencies. His main program is known as the "AAA Accelerator" (AI Automation Agency Accelerator).

## Key aspects of Liam Ottley's courses include:

*   **Curriculum:** The AAA Accelerator covers various aspects of building an AI agency, such as AI agents and workflow automation, sales psychology, client acquisition strategies (cold email, LinkedIn, YouTube), business operations, and client delivery.
*   **Support:** The program emphasizes extensive support, offering unlimited 1-on-1 coaching in areas like development, sales, personal branding, outreach, and business strategy. It also includes weekly coaching sessions with Liam Ottley and a dedicated success manager.
*   **Free Resources:** Liam Ottley provides free educational content on platforms like YouTube and within his "AI Automation Agency Hub" Skool community. These resources often guide beginners on how to establish an AI agency, develop AI skills, and secure initial clients.
*   **Target Audience:** His courses are designed for aspiring AI entrepreneurs, including those without prior coding experience, who wish to leverage the opportunities in the AI industry.

It is worth noting that there have been mixed reviews regarding the AAA Accelerator, with some negative experiences reported, citing a "pushy" sales process and difficulties with refunds.
