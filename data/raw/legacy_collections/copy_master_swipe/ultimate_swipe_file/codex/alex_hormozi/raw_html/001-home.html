FAQs

What is the Acquisition.com Scaling Workshop?

It's not an event. It's a workshop. At my office.

Expect zero motivational talks. Meaning - we get tactical.

On day one, we break down how we fast track growth, recruit top talent, and frameworks we use for "high return" decision making.

This is the same stuff we use in our portfolio.

On day two, we get hands on. We separate into small groups.

You meet with our directors (specialized in marketing, sales, people, profit, and strategy) to get personalized help.

 

How to register for a workshop

To register for the Acqusition.com Scaling Workshop, we require you to meet minimum requirements. Our Workshop Sales Team would love to talk to you about those requirements. Click here to get started!

Who is the right fit for a workshop?

To determine if you are the right fit for the Acqusition.com Scaling Workshop, book a call to speak with a member of our workshop concierge team. We require you to meet some minimum entry requirements to be allowed in the room. These requirements include but are not limited to being active in business, a revenue threshold and operating in an industry where we are confident we can provide value. If this is of interest, book a call today to see if you are the right fit.

Who are the main speakers?

You will be introduced to the team that actively runs our portfolio. In addition, on the second day, you can look forward to breakout sessions with Acquisition.com subject matter experts so that no question is left unanswered by the time you leave.
