*The people who give away their best thinking attract the best clients.*

**Step 1: Map the Repetition.** Track every task your team does more than 3x per week. If a human does it the same way every time, a machine can do it.

**Step 2: Find the Handoffs.** Every time information moves from one person or tool to another, there's a leak. Count the handoffs.

**Step 3: Time the Bottlenecks.** How long do decisions sit waiting? Measure the gap between "ready for approval" and "approved."

**Step 4: Calculate the Invisible Cost.** Multiply wasted hours by fully-loaded hourly rate. This number will make your CFO's eye twitch.

**Step 5: Prioritize by Impact-to-Effort.** Stack rank by: what saves the most time with the least complexity? Start there. Build momentum.

You can run this audit yourself. Some of our best clients did exactly that -- then realized they wanted someone to build the assembly line, not just draw the blueprint.

Either way, you win.
